About — (00)

I design experiences where design, sustainability, technology and human experience meet.

Born in Brussels, raised in Mechelen and shaped over the past five years as a Product Development engineer in Antwerp, I've gradually positioned myself at the intersection of people, technology and the planet.

Sustainability has been the constant thread — from climate activism in school to designing for circularity today. It's why I started Product Development in the first place: I believe design is one of the most powerful levers we have to make a positive change.

I see design — and scenography — as more than objects or spaces. It's the design of an experience that begins long before someone arrives and continues long after they leave: branding, atmosphere, sound, light, storytelling, anticipation and memory, all part of the same narrative.

I work best in multidisciplinary teams built on trust, complementary skills and a shared purpose. I bring energy, ideas and momentum — and I'm never afraid to connect people and start things.

01 Ergonomics · Branding

B|YOU

A jeweler's tool family

  • Year2023
  • RoleGroup & solo
  • FieldErgonomics & branding

A cohesive family of four ergonomic hand tools for the hobbyist jeweler, built around a strong, dreamy brand. After studying jewelers and defining a Gen X audience (43–61), the winning concept paired a tiltable, comfortable grip with a precise tensioning system.

  • Ergonomics
  • Branding
  • Tool family
  • User research
  • SolidWorks
Optimal

Grip sits at the pressure point between middle, ring and pinky fingers.

Modular

A sliding frame fits any saw-blade length.

Ergonomic

Handle tilts upward, cutting wrist torsion in long sessions.

Functional

A tightening system locks and tensions the blade with ease.

04 Master's thesis

Repair Aid

Repair, don't replace.

  • Year2025—2026
  • RoleSolo · Master's thesis
  • PromotorsJ. Saldien · P. Van Pelt

My master's thesis on the future of repair. Repair Aid is a digital repair companion and product-service system that guides citizens from a vague “it stopped working” to a clear diagnosis and a realistic repair path — turning a throw-away reflex into a confident, do-able next step.

  • Circular economy
  • Service design
  • AI agents
  • Diagnosis
  • E6 ecosystem
  • Antwerp

Built around small household electronics — starting with the vacuum cleaner — Repair Aid turns the user into a sensor through safe visual, audio and maintenance checks, then weighs cost, time, complexity, success-chance and lifespan gained on a single decision screen. Its physical counterpart, Repair Base, offers guided hands-on repair when a home fix is too uncertain.

  • 35 Mte-waste in Europe each year
  • ~22%correctly collected & recycled
  • R5repair sits high on the R-ladder

Positioned within the European E6 repair ecosystem (Interreg North-West Europe), exploring how AI agents can bridge user, device, repairer and data.

05 Scenography · Events

Firma Funk

SILO 1 — a party that feels like a narrative

  • Year2025
  • RoleCo-founder & facilitator
  • FieldScenography & event design

Firma Funk is a collective I co-founded with four friends — bringing together product development, graphic design, photography, journalism, economics and social work. Our first event, SILO 1, transformed Bar Ziek, a former hospital site in Antwerp, into a temporary world built around vinyl, groove and a gradual journey from day into night.

  • Scenography
  • Event design
  • Vinyl & groove
  • Inclusivity
  • Community
  • Co-founding

Scenography is more than decoration — it's music, light, space, interaction and the emotional rhythm of a day.

SILO 1 also hosted a wearable haptic prototype, letting people with hearing impairments feel the music — proof that cultural experiences can be more inclusive without losing their artistic value.
