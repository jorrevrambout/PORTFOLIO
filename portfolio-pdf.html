Jorre VramboutLetter — 03

What is your vision of scenography today?

I see scenography as something that extends far beyond spatial design alone. It is about creating a complete experience that begins long before the event, through identity, vision and anticipation. The story continues through interactivity, originality and atmosphere, with the visitor's freedom and engagement at the centre. For me, a successful experience lives on in the thoughts and conversations of its participants long afterwards.

How will you divide your time next year?

Half of my time will go to the RepairHub Antwerp project, a collaboration between the University of Antwerp and Stormkop, designing an inclusive repair ecosystem. In parallel, this may become the starting point of a PhD on Agentic AI in circular repair. Alongside these commitments, I hope to develop my knowledge of scenography through this programme, meet like minded people and build a pathway into the event industry.

Jorre VramboutLetter — 03

What would you build if budget were not a factor?

A multi day underground festival in an abandoned industrial site in Charleroi. Intimate and immersive, with climbing structures, greenery and projection mapping instead of screens. The focus would be on emerging local artists performing live, with visitors discovering secret stages that tell a story from the 1960s to the 1990s.

Inclusivity and small scale would remain central. The architecture itself would become an active part of the experience, with water, heights, underground spaces and hidden passages reshaping the visitor journey at every turn.

Selected workBiloo

2024 / Bachelor's thesis

Biloo

The composting toilet

An off grid, waterless duo compost toilet that improves comfort and sustainability at crowded music festivals. Fully mechanical, with no electronics at all.

jorrevrambout@gmail.com02 — Biloo15
BilooHow it works

Two cabins share one 330 litre sawdust dispenser and a single waste tank. A manual flush releases a precise amount of sawdust through a custom rotor profile to control odor and moisture. A robust AISI 304 stainless steel frame and weather resistant HPL panels handle heavy festival use, and the design can be transported, stacked and placed with standard forklifts, so there is no on site assembly.

100people per toilet per day

0litres of water

Selected workRepair Aid

2025 / 2026 — Master's thesis

Repair Aid

Repair, don't replace.

My master's thesis investigating agentic AI in product development. A digital tool that empowers consumers to diagnose and repair their household electronics.

jorrevrambout@gmail.com04 — Repair Aid19
Repair AidFrom uncertainty to a route

Many devices are thrown away while repair is still possible. The problem is often not the fault but the uncertainty around it. Repair Aid combines historical repair data, user input and agentic AI to guide people step by step from a vague fault to a clear diagnosis and a realistic repair route.

Promotors prof. dr. Jelle Saldien and Philip Van Pelt, Thomas More, within the European E6 ecosystem.

jorrevrambout@gmail.com04 — Repair Aid20
Repair AidThree routes to a repair

Three routes to a repair

Repair Aid distinguishes independent repair at home, guided repair in a Repair Base and professional repair, then suggests the most feasible option based on the situation, the complexity of the fault and the expected effort.

Firma Funk

SILO 1, a party that feels like a narrative

An events collective I cofounded with four friends, focused on intimate events that put atmosphere, connection and storytelling before spectacle.

jorrevrambout@gmail.com05 — Firma Funk22
Firma FunkSILO 1 at Bar Ziek

We transformed Bar Ziek, a former hospital site in Antwerp, into a temporary world of vinyl and groove that moved from day into night. Scenography here is more than decoration. It is music, light, space, interaction and the emotional rhythm of a day.

SILO 1 also hosted a wearable haptic prototype that let people with hearing impairments feel the music, showing how events can be more inclusive without losing their artistic value.
